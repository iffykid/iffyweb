ABOUT ME

here is a little information about me

Hi!

My name is Shaun a Computer Science and Graphic Designer. I have been interested in computer science for several years and have completed a number of courses in subjects such as artificial intelligence, software engineering, and data structures and algorithms.

In addition to my studies, I have also gained experience as a graphic designer, where I have worked on projects such as designing logos and banners, and rebranding company identities. I am proficient in a number of technical skills, including Java, HTML/CSS, and Adobe Photoshop, and am also fluent in English and and speak Afrikaans and Setswana.

I am passionate about using my skills and knowledge to create useful and innovative projects, such as my school project, a mobile application for alerting authorities of crimes in university communities. In my free time, I enjoy working on personal projects, such as building e-commerce websites and managing online businesses.
